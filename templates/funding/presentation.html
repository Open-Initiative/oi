What is Open Funding?

Open Funding is the funding platform for all your open source projects. Contribute to the projects, and see the progress you make. Be part of the project on the long run!

Follow the evolution of the projects

With Open Funding you can see the features that are proposed by or to the developers. You can see the progress made on them and know when they are coming.

Give your opinion

Propose new features, vote for the one you look forward to having, and foremost provide the money that enables them.

Get involved

Now you can have an influence on your open source project, and be part of the game. Open source is never obscure anymore.
