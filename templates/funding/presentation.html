{% trans "What is Open Funding?" %}

{% trans "Description" %}

{% trans "

Open Funding is the crowdfunding platform for all your free software. Here you can help fund the features of free software projects proposed by developers, by contributing and following their progress.

Unlike platforms that offer donations to the projects, Open Funding proposes to fund a service delivery. Developers are committed to their results and may get several features funded over time. This provides a new business model for free software developers.

Whether you're a user or developer this site is for you! Open Funding is the intermediate between those who look for free software solutions and those who have projects to develop and are looking for funding.

With Open Funding, you can contribute and support free software development. Be part of the projects on the long run and tell us the projects you’d like to see funded!

" %}

{% trans "Why free software?" %}

{% trans "

Free software has its origins in the 1980s, when some computer users wanted to be able to change the software that run these computers. So they authorized the users to modify their programs and redistribute them. Many people started to contribute to the software, and it quickly became clear that this system had many advantages.

Today free software appears as a credible model that has proved its efficiency. By reusing what has been done by others, developers can quickly create new applications and contribute to the common good that free software is. But these developers need to live. Today many developers sell services around their software, but these revenues are not directly related to the development work itself.

Open Funding allows you to participate in free software development by helping to fund the features you need to see added to the free software you use. Today, software is a foundation of our society. There is no machine running without software. The software is simply a recipe that makes many areas of our activities run.

At Open Initiative, we believe that this is everyone's business, and it should not be left to financial interests or some enlightened technicians. Open Funding allows you to participate in the construction of free software if you are a developer who wants to live of your work or a user who wants software finally fitting your needs.

" %}

{% trans "How does it work?" %}

{% trans "The principle" %}

{% trans "

The developers propose their free software projects on the platform with a list of features and the amount of money they need to develop each of them. Users will fund the features with the amount they want according to their interests. Once the requested amount is reached, the project owner can start the development. The developer determines the delivery date when the project is finished. Users then have 14 days to validate the result or request corrections. The developer is paid only after validation by the users.

This way Open Funding secures the conclusion of the project to the users and the payment to developers once the work is done.

Open Funding fees are limited to a 5% commission deducted from the amount paid to the beneficiary when the mission is completed. The payment only happens in case the users validate the project, so that the software is guaranteed to answer your needs.

" %}

{% trans "How does it work?" %}

{% trans "Creating a project" %}

{% trans "

If you have a free software project, you propose it on the platform with a list of its features. You explain your project and its features in the creation page, and for each feature you determine the amount required for their development. You can add several features for each project. We add our commission to this sum: 5% exclusive of taxes.

I created a project, what now?

Before publishing your project on the platform we will contact you by email and then by telephone. We wish to establish contact with project owners to learn a bit more from them directly, and assist them in the way they will promote the project.

Once we have validated this together, it’s time to share your project with the community. Each developer is free to communicate as they wish and transmit the information to users to promote and fund the project. However, we are always available to guide you through this process.

You can contact us: contact@openinititiative.com

I gathered the funds, what now?

When a feature reaches the estimated amount, you can start the development. The development time is specified in the description of evolution. At any time, you can communicate with funders on the platform to discuss its progress.

Once the development is done, you deliver the product. Each developer determines with his funders the best way to deliver the result. The product can be integrated into the next version of the software, but it can also be a fork, be delivered as a patch, etc.

Users have 14 days to accept or request corrections to the product. If after 14 days there there’s no validation of a funder, the product will be validated automatically and the developer will be paid. Each time a user validates the development, the amount will be paid to the developer.

Open Funding receives 5% commission excluding taxes of the feature’s total cost.

" %}

{% trans "Funding a project" %}

{% trans "

Open Funding is the place to fund free software projects created by developers, feature by feature. On the site we receive projects of different categories of free software. You can find the latest projects on the home page.

Why should I fund a project?

Free software is free as in “free speech,” not as in “free beer”. Projects that you find on Open Funding are created by developers who dedicate their time and efforts to development. Funding a project on Open Funding is bringing a solution to your free software needs and rewarding developers to the level they deserve.

You can follow the progress of the projects you support and contact the creator at any time. You validate the product when it is finished and confirm that the result is as expected.

Funding feature by feature

On Open Funding, you can find features that improve your favorite free software. Once you create an account on the website, you are ready to participate. If there is a feature of a project that interests you, click on the 'support' button and you set the amount you want to help fund. When you fund features you accept the Terms of Use.

You have credit in your account that allows you to fund projects on the site. You can increase your credit at any time in the My Account section. This way you help funding your favorite projects with the amount according to your interests. You can modify you credit at any time.

Our secured system allows you to keep your account and funding secure.

Propose a new feature

You can make or suggest new features to a project you like. On the project page you can add a new feature with its description and relevant information. The developer then accepts or modifies your proposal. You will be notified of any change to your suggestion.

" %}

{% trans "License and fees" %}

{% trans "

License

All projects published on Open Funding are distributed with a free license. Each developer must specify the license under which his development will be placed.

Fees

As an intermediary between developers and users, Open Funding receives a commission of 5% excluding tax of the total cost of the funded development. This fee is charged to developers once the amount determined for the feature is achieved. Open Funding does not charge if the feature doesn’t reach the expected funding.

" %}
